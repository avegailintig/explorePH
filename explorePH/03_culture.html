Culture & Travel Tips

Respectful travel makes better memories. Here are some quick tips and cultural notes.

Travel Advice

  • Bring sunscreen, insect repellent, and a water bottle — hydration matters.
  • Check weather and local transport options in advance.
  • Book accommodations early for peak travel season.

Cultural Etiquette

  • Use polite language — terms like "po" and "opo" show respect to elders.
  • Ask permission before taking photos of people or private property.
  • Be mindful of local customs — dress modestly in religious sites.

Safety & Practical Tips

  • Keep copies of IDs and travel documents.
  • Use accredited tour operators for activities like diving and island hopping.
  • Respect nature — don’t leave trash behind and follow guidelines for marine parks.
