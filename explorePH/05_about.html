About Us

ExplorePH is a small, community-first travel collective made up of passionate local guides, storytellers, and photographers. We organize responsible and authentic experiences that connect visitors with the people, places, and traditions of the Philippines.

Our Mission

To create meaningful travel experiences that support local communities, preserve culture, and celebrate the natural beauty of the Philippines. We design tours and itineraries that are sustainable, immersive, and respectful of the places we visit.

What We Do

  • Guided day tours and multi-day itineraries tailored to small groups.
  • Itinerary planning and logistics for independent travelers.
  • Photography support and guided photo-walks.
  • Community-led cultural experiences and workshops.
