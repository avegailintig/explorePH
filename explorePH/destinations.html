Top Philippine Destinations

Handpicked locations to suit every travel style — beaches, mountains, history, and adventure.

Palawan — El Nido & Coron

Iconic limestone cliffs, secret lagoons, and world-class snorkeling.

Siargao — Surf & Island Life

Perfect waves for surfers, island hopping, and relaxed seaside eateries.

Baguio — Cool Highlands

Pine-scented air, parks, and cozy cafés — a cool escape from the lowlands.

Cebu — History & Waterfalls

A city of contrasts — urban life, historical sites, and natural wonders nearby.

Bohol — Chocolate Hills & Tarsiers

Rolling limestone hills, gentle river cruises, and unique wildlife encounters.

Banaue — Rice Terraces

Millennia-old terraces carved into the mountains — a cultural and scenic wonder.

Vigan — Heritage Streets

Cobblestone lanes, Spanish-era houses, and timeless charm in Ilocos Sur.

Siquijor — Beaches & Mystique

Laid-back island vibes, waterfalls, and clear snorkel spots with a mystical reputation.

Batanes — Rugged Beauty

Wind-swept cliffs, rolling hills, and a distinct Ivatan culture at the northern edge of the Philippines.

Iloilo & Guimaras — Food & Islands

Rich heritage architecture, lively festivals, and mango-sweet island beaches nearby.

Davao — Nature & Mount Apo

Gateway to Mount Apo, lush parks, and a vibrant durian-loving city culture.

Tagaytay — Taal Views

Cool breezes and postcard views of Taal Lake and Volcano — perfect for day trips from Manila.

Zambales — Surf & Beach Camping

Sandy coves, surf breaks, and popular camping spots like Nagsasa and Anawangin.

Camiguin — Island of Fire

Small island packed with waterfalls, hot springs, and volcanic landscapes.

Apo Reef — Diving Paradise

One of the largest coral reef systems in the world — excellent diving and marine biodiversity.
